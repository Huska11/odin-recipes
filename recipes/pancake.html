pancake

how make home made pancake recipes under 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ teaspoon maple flavored extract
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine white sugar, brown sugar, and water; bring to a boil and cook for 3 minutes
  2. Stir in maple extract and vanilla, remove from heat, and let cool.
  3. Serve at room temperature and store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
