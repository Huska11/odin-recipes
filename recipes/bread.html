bread

This bread machine recipe is easy and foolproof. It makes a very soft and tasty loaf of bread with a flaky crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package bread machine yeast
  • Â¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place water, sugar, and yeast in the pan of the bread machine
  3. Let yeast dissolve and foam for 10 minutes.
  4. Add oil, flour, and salt to the yeast. Select Basic or White Bread setting, and press Start.
  5. Remove the loaf from the machine, cool 5 minutes in the pan, then transfer to a rack to cool completely.
  6. Slice, serve, and enjoy!
