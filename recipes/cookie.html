cookie

these cookies are soft and chewy.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar, divided
  • ¾ cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • ¼ cup molasses
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt and ½ teaspoon ground cloves

steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix 1 cup sugar, butter, and egg together in a medium bowl until smooth.
  3. Stir in molasses.
  4. Bake in batches in the preheated oven until tops are cracked, about 8 to 10 minutes.
  5. Cool on wire racks. Enjoy!
